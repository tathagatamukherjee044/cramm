Privacy Policy - cramm.in

cramm.in is an open-source application distributed under the MIT License. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle the information you provide while using our services.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how cramm.in collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your information.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Email Address: To create and manage your user account.
  • Submitted Questions: To improve the application's question bank and provide a collaborative learning environment.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • To create and manage your user account using your email address.
  • To improve the application with submitted questions.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not share your personal information with any third parties.

5. Data Security

We take the following measures to protect your data:

  • Access controls to limit who can see your data.
  • Secure servers to store your data safely.

6. User Rights

Currently, users have the right to access their submitted questions. In the future, we will implement the ability to delete user accounts and associated data.

7. Cookies

We use cookies for authentication purposes only. These cookies help us to maintain your session and ensure you stay logged in securely.

8. Children's Privacy

We do not have specific age restrictions for using our application. We collect email addresses from all users for account creation purposes.

9. International Data Transfers

We do not transfer user data internationally.

10. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of any changes to this Privacy Policy by posting the updated policy on our application and, if necessary, via email.
